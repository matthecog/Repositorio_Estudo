# 📍 Contoso Pizza – Lisboa, Portugal 🇵🇹

**Description:**  
Nestled in the historic Alfama neighborhood, Contoso Pizza Lisboa blends traditional Portuguese charm with Italian culinary excellence. The azulejo-decorated walls and stunning views of the Tagus River create an unforgettable dining experience. Signature pizzas like the "Bacalhau Delícia" with salted cod and the "Alentejo Pork" celebrate local flavors, while classic options satisfy purists. Enjoy a glass of Alentejo wine on the terrace as you watch the sunset over the city's iconic red rooftops.
