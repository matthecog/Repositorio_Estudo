# 📍 Contoso Pizza – Curitiba, Brazil 🇧🇷

**Description:**  
Situated in the green and innovative city of Curitiba, Contoso Pizza reflects the city's European heritage and sustainability focus. Located near the iconic Oscar Niemeyer Museum, this eco-friendly location features solar panels, composting programs, and locally-sourced organic ingredients. Try the "Araucária" pizza with pine nuts and local herbs, or the "Barreado Inspired" with slow-cooked beef. The heated outdoor garden is perfect for Curitiba's cooler climate, and the artisanal beer selection showcases the best of Paraná's craft breweries.
