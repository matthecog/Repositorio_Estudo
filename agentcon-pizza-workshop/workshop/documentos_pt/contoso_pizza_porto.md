# 📍 Contoso Pizza – Porto, Portugal 🇵🇹

**Description:**  
Overlooking the iconic Ribeira district, Contoso Pizza Porto offers a rustic yet modern dining experience along the Douro River. The exposed granite walls and warm lighting create a cozy atmosphere perfect for enjoying artisan pizzas. Try the "Francesinha Fusion" inspired by Porto's famous sandwich, or the "Tripeiro Special" with local cured meats. Pair your meal with a refreshing glass of Vinho Verde or a robust Port wine for dessert. Live Fado music on weekends adds to the authentic Portuguese experience.
