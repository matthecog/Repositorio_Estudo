# 📍 Contoso Pizza – Belo Horizonte, Brazil 🇧🇷

**Description:**  
In the heart of Minas Gerais, Contoso Pizza Belo Horizonte celebrates the region's famous culinary traditions with a pizza twist. Located in the bohemian Savassi district, this location features a modern industrial design with local art on the walls. The "Mineiro" pizza with queijo minas and linguiça is a local favorite, while the "Pão de Queijo" appetizer pizza puts a creative spin on the beloved Brazilian cheese bread. Known for generous portions and warm hospitality that reflects the Mineiro spirit.
