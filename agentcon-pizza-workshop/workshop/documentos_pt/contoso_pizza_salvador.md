# 📍 Contoso Pizza – Salvador, Brazil 🇧🇷

**Description:**  
In the colorful Pelourinho historic district, Contoso Pizza Salvador brings together African, Portuguese, and Italian culinary traditions. The colonial building features vibrant colors, high ceilings, and balconies overlooking the cobblestone streets. Signature pizzas include the "Acarajé Fusion" with vatapá-inspired toppings and the "Bahia Tropical" with dendê oil drizzle and local seafood. The sounds of axé music fill the air, and on Tuesdays, join the famous Pelourinho street celebrations right outside. A true taste of Bahian culture.
